MY CREATIVE JOURNEY

It all started when I was just a kid, barely 9 years old, armed with nothing but my dad's old laptop and a spark of curiosity. I tinkered away on MS Paint, experimenting with shapes and colors, completely unaware that I was laying the foundation for my future career.
At the tender age of 11, I stumbled upon CorelDRAW, and that's when the magic truly began. It was like discovering a whole new universe of possibilities right at my fingertips. I poured hours into honing my craft, mastering every tool and technique until I could wield them with finesse.
By the time I turned 14, I was already a maestro in CorelDRAW. But I craved more. I yearned to push the boundaries of my creativity further. So, I dived headfirst into the vast ocean of Photoshop and Illustrator, watching tutorials on YouTube like a hungry artist in search of inspiration.
Fast forward to my 17th year, bought with a new laptop and an insatiable hunger for knowledge, I embarked on yet another adventure – the world of 3D design in Blender3D. With each tutorial watched and course completed, I felt myself growing not just as a designer but as a creator.
And here I am today, at 18, with two years of professional experience under my belt, eager to take on the world of graphic design and 3D with even more gusto. My journey has been filled with twists and turns, challenges and triumphs, but through it all, one thing remains constant – my unwavering passion for creativity.
